Biography

I am a fifth-year Ph.D. candidate in Department of Computer Science, University of California, Los Angeles, working with Prof. Carlo Zaniolo and Prof. Yizhou Sun. My research interests include natural language processing, knowledge graphs, and question answering. Before joining UCLA as a Ph.D. student, I received my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science in June 2016 from Special Class for the Gifted Young at Xi'an Jiao Tong University.

Research Interests

My research interests include knowledge graphs, natural language processing and data mining (graphs).
